What I built

Seven Builds. One Floor.

Each one of these shipped, and each one was made better by a conversation with someone on the team. [Tags] are the verb I want each project to be remembered for.

Oki v1 — RFID-personalised chatbot

The first visitor-facing chatbot at ParSEC, with RFID-driven personalisation: tap a card, and the conversation knows who you are and what you came for. The architecture that everything since has been built on.

RFIDLLM PersonalisationKiosk

[Grasp]

RoArm M2-S — depth-aware pick

Pairing classical homography with the Depth-Anything monocular-depth model, the arm picks objects at arbitrary depths inside its workspace using only a single RGB camera. The foundation for the upcoming ROSE-ARM module.

OpenCVPyTorch Depth-AnythingInverse Kinematics
[Move]

Go2 Phase 1 — owned autonomy stack

Reverse-engineered the Unitree protocol and rebuilt the autonomy stack from the ground up: live LiDAR occupancy mapping, A* planning, a holonomic pure-pursuit follower, three-tier stuck recovery. A robot we fully understand and fully control.

ROS 2FastAPI SLAMA* Planning Jetson Orin
[Welcome]

Project Octopus — Oki + Onsite

The production visitor-AI platform: a press-and-hold kiosk experience for visitors and a full-stack admin tool for curators. Continuously deployed to Vercel — every commit is live on every kiosk within seconds.

Next.jsTypeScript PostgresVercel Voice Pipeline

Onsite-Faci — the facilitator surface

A companion to Onsite, tuned for the facilitator on the floor. Live status of the kiosks, gallery context at a tap, structured observation logging into the foundation's data infrastructure.

[Patrol]

Go2 Phase 2 — cooperative dual-brain

A novel architecture: the vendor's USLAM handles patrol, our independent Jetson layer handles perception and interaction. The robot patrols, sees a visitor, pauses, greets, and resumes — fully autonomous, all coordinated over ROS 2.

ROS 2MediaPipe State MachineTailscale Thermal Watchdog
[Compose]

AEP Storyboard Pipeline — for the content team

Built alongside Pramod and the P.A.I.R Lab team. A Cowork plugin that takes a script and emits a verified storyboard with sourced Envato / VideoHive assets. About 40% off the content team's special-effects workload — and zero hallucinated URLs by design.
